# Property, Construction and Infrastructure

**Status:** Approved baseline

## Rights

Separate plot ownership from resource rights, leases, mortgages, easements, public restrictions and building ownership where law permits. Transfers are registered and auditable.

Players and entities may buy, sell, rent, lease, mortgage and develop property. Speculation is allowed and carries market, financing and policy risk.

## Buildings

Buildings are modular abstractions, not navigable room simulations. A building definition specifies footprint, use, base capacity and compatible modules.

Examples include houses, apartments, farms, shops, factories, warehouses, schools, hospitals, public-service buildings, power plants and organization halls.

Construction quality emerges from designs, materials, workers, tools, site conditions and contractor capability. The player does not select quality directly.

## Plot capacity

Plots have buildable capacity constrained by terrain, access, utilities, zoning and footprint. Exact plot and module dimensions remain an open scale decision.

## Construction process

Construction requires approved rights, design, materials, labour, equipment, energy, access and time. Projects progress through versioned stages so cancellation, delay, inspection and partial loss are representable.

## Infrastructure

Road, rail, port, airport and utility projects are owned and funded by permitted private or public actors. Infrastructure changes movement, capacity and land value.

Municipalities may procure construction rather than magically placing completed assets. Emergency simplifications for NPC bootstrap must still create costs and provenance.

## Maintenance

Buildings and infrastructure have condition. Usage, age, environment and initial quality create maintenance demand. Deferred maintenance lowers capacity, safety or speed before catastrophic failure.
