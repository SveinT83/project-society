# Plots, Geology and Resources

**Status:** Approved baseline

## Plot state

Every accessible plot has a stable identity and persistent state. A plot may describe:

- coordinates, region and accessibility
- surface terrain, slope, water and climate
- soil composition and condition
- vegetation coverage and renewable-resource potential
- pollution and recent extraction pressure
- ownership, rights, restrictions and improvements
- buildings and infrastructure capacity
- known observations and surveys

A plot is not a single resource number. It is the intersection of layered world systems.

## Soil

Soil may include proportions of sand, clay, silt, stone and organic matter plus pH, moisture, drainage, fertility and contamination. Farming changes condition through cultivation, crop choice, fertilisation, erosion and rest.

Players can improve poor ground, but transformation costs labour, materials, equipment and time. Soil type constrains what is efficient; it does not reduce farming to a binary fertile/unfertile flag.

## Deposits

Mineral veins, ore bodies, coal seams, oil and gas fields are separate spatial entities crossing multiple plots and depths. A deposit records geometry, host geology, composition, grade distribution, depth, recoverable quantity and extraction constraints.

Players initially see geological indicators, not exact reserves. Prospecting, surveys and drilling create observations with method-dependent accuracy and uncertainty. Survey information is an ownable, shareable and sellable asset.

Extraction reduces the deposit's recoverable quantity. Minerals, oil and gas do not regenerate.

## Renewable state

Forest, wildlife and fish are aggregate plot or local-area values, not individual organisms.

- Forest coverage changes through harvesting, regrowth and spread from suitable neighbours.
- Hunting results use local habitat, nearby habitat, recent pressure, pollution, skill and equipment.
- Fishing results use local potential, recent catch pressure, pollution, season, weather, skill and equipment.

Periodic jobs update coarse state. Detailed results are calculated when an action occurs.

## Discovery and information

The authoritative deposit is not automatically visible. Store observations separately from truth. A character's view is assembled from authorised observations, recency, ownership, public data and shared data.

## Resource extraction invariant

Every extraction consumes:

- lawful or unlawful access to the location and resource right
- character or NPC time
- suitable equipment and its durability
- energy or fuel when applicable
- extractable quantity

It produces material batches, provenance, quality, waste, environmental effects and skill XP.
