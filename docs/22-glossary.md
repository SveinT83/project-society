# Glossary

**Status:** Approved terminology baseline

- **Account:** Authentication identity controlling one active player character.
- **Action:** Server-authoritative timed activity performed by a character.
- **Actor:** Character or legal entity capable of authorised commands.
- **Batch:** Fungible quantity sharing type, quality and provenance summary.
- **Character:** Persistent player or NPC person in the world.
- **Company:** Profit-seeking legal entity separate from its owners.
- **Constitution:** Highest-level, durable product laws.
- **Deposit:** Spatial, finite underground resource entity that may cross plots.
- **Domain event:** Recorded fact resulting from an accepted state change.
- **Government:** Territorial public authority with legally defined powers.
- **Information/knowledge:** What an actor is permitted and able to know, distinct from authoritative truth.
- **NPC:** Persistent non-player character maintaining population and minimum functions.
- **Observation:** Time-stamped, source-bound record about part of the world.
- **Organization:** Member-based group such as a party, religion, union or club.
- **Plot:** Stable coordinate cell in the two-dimensional world grid.
- **Provenance:** Traceable origin and transformation history.
- **Quality:** Relevant measurable characteristics produced by inputs and process, not a purchased tier.
- **Recipe/method:** Versioned production definition.
- **Region:** Spatial partition used for geography, government and bounded simulation.
- **Skill:** Activity-specific learned capability.
- **World truth:** Authoritative state, whether or not any player knows it.
